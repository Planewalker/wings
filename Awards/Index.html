I've always sort of had mixed emotions about seeing web awards on other sites. First off, it kind of seems like bragging; "Lookie at what I gots me!" and all that. Sure, I'll admit that most sites deserve most of those awards, but still... Which kind of leads into the second thing I felt - jealousy. What did they have that I didn't? (This question became even more valid after I redesigned this site into its current form.)

But now, I get to join the ranks of the awarded and cause others to continue to feel as I did. :) I don't know how much worth these have in the long run, or to you (the current web viewer), but here they are. They at least make me feel better.
